Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how our application ("App", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our employee attendance system. By using this application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Our application may collect the following information:

  • Employee name and Employee ID
  • Precise GPS location
  • Attendance records
  • Check-in and check-out history
  • Application usage logs

2. Background & Foreground Location

Our application collects precise location information in both foreground and background, including when the application is closed or not actively in use.

Location tracking is a core feature of the application and is required for automatic attendance management.

The collected location is used to:

  • Automatically mark employee attendance.
  • Verify that employees are inside their assigned attendance zone.
  • Detect entry and exit from authorized work locations.
  • Prevent fake or fraudulent attendance.
  • Maintain accurate attendance records.

The application may access location 24 hours a day to ensure attendance events are detected whenever an employee enters or exits an authorized attendance zone.

We do not use location information for advertising, marketing, or selling user data.

3. Battery Optimization

To ensure uninterrupted automatic attendance, the application may request users to disable battery optimization.

Many Android devices restrict background services to save battery power. Disabling battery optimization allows the application to:

  • Continue monitoring attendance zones.
  • Run required background services.
  • Prevent missed attendance due to system restrictions.
  • Provide accurate automatic check-in and check-out.

Disabling battery optimization is requested solely for attendance management. The application does not misuse this capability for unrelated background activities.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide automatic attendance.
  • Verify employee presence.
  • Manage attendance history.
  • Generate attendance reports.
  • Improve application performance.
  • Prevent attendance fraud.
  • Provide technical support.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Your information may only be shared with:

  • Your employer or organization.
  • Authorized administrators.
  • Government authorities when legally required.
  • Trusted service providers who assist in operating the application.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Data Retention

Attendance records and location information are retained only as long as necessary to provide attendance services or comply with legal or organizational requirements.

8. User Rights

Users may:

  • Request access to their personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of personal data where permitted by law.
  • Manage app permissions through device settings.

Please note that disabling location permission or background location access may prevent automatic attendance from functioning correctly.

9. Permissions Used

The application may request the following permissions:

  • Fine Location
  • Background Location
  • Foreground Service
  • Foreground Service Location
  • Activity Recognition (if supported)
  • Internet Access
  • Network State
  • Wake Lock
  • Ignore Battery Optimizations
  • Boot Completed (to restart attendance service after device reboot)
  • Notifications (to display foreground service notification)
